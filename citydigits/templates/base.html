CityDigits

{% endblock %}
{% block contents %}

City Digits is a collaborative project among MIT’s Civic Data Design Project, Brooklyn’s Center for Urban Pedagogy, and CUNY’s Brooklyn College, to develop and pilot test innovative resources and tools that support high school students’ learning of mathematics.

City Digits is designing two, place-based curricular modules to investigate and analyze specific themes related to the local, urban context. The curricular modules will be enhanced by the integration of geo-spatial technologies that enable students to explore local their urban landscape, collect field data, and organize and visualize patterns. Our first module is about state lotteries, with pilot testing scheduled for April 2013.
